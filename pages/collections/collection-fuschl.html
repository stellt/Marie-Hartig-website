Fuschl Rosewood

I was commissioned to come up with a design for this Bar / living room area. A warm yet elegant space in the oldest part of the hotel - a 15th century tower. The Schloss Fuschl is a stunning castle perfectly positioned right on the shore of a large turquoise lake not too far from Salzburg. The surrounding area, mountains and lush forests, is pristine nature at its best. The new owners of the hotel also have their very own private curated Old Masters art collection that I needed to consider when creating the design of the walls. The composition had to work with and for them: complementing, enhancing while at the same time allowing the wall to be an artwork in itself.

Location: Hotel Bar. Fuschl, Austria
