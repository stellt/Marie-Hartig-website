Rooftop Pool

This fabulous private roof terrace in a stunning apartment in the heart of Vienna, has been decked out with a new gorgeous view. Initially I painted this canopy on a 1.50 x 5 meter canvas in my studio.

It was then photographed with total precision and after some trial and error and color correction it was finally printed on this large waterproof canvas and installed in a light frame — so that you can switch it on and enjoy the view at night… naturally.

Location: Pool Terrace. Private Home. Vienna, Austria
